Research

My research work

Title: Drowsiness Driver Detection Using Neural Network on UTA-RLDD Dataset

Publisher: STI 2020 Green University Bangladesh (Sponsored by IEEE) Â· Dec 19, 2020

Road accidents are very common term in developing countries like Bangladesh. On of the biggest reason behind these clashes are drowsiness or carelessness of driver while driving. Drowsiness in driving refers to a situation where driver while driving is feeling tiredness or sleepiness. However, loss of so many valuable lives can be rescued by detecting drowsiness level of a driver and alerting him. For this purpose, we propose a model using neural network that can detect drowsiness of a driver by extracting facial landmarks to detect face, calculate eye aspect ratio (EAR), mouth aspect ratio(MAR), mouth over eye aspect ratio (MOEAR), pupil circularity (PC). A realistic drowsiness dataset named UTA RLDD which is the largest available drowsiness dataset has been used for our research. Our proposed model includes two very popular neural network model named Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) and Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). Both are complex model which gives less false values. In our model CNN gives a better accuracy 71.9% where LSTM gives accuracy of 64.1%.
